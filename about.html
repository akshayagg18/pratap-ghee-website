The Man Who Would Offer
Nothing but the Best

Pratap Ghee is an initiative of Late Sh. Pyare Lal ‘Ghee Wale’, who belonged to village Dahar near Panipat in Haryana. In 1958, he started the Desi Ghee business from Khari Baoli, Delhi.

A former chairman of MCD Shahdara, New Delhi, Sh. Pyare Lal was a deeply spiritual man who believed that nothing but the best should be offered to God. Ghee is an essential ingredient of all Hindu rituals, and he insisted it be served most piously. In 2004 he took sanyas from grihastha ashram, and lived in Rishikesh for the rest of his life.

Ghee is Amrit, and adulterating it is the same as poisoning.
Sh. Pyare Lal ‘Ghee Wale’, Founder

Pratap Ghee has cherished its clients’ faith and unwavering trust for over six decades. In the Indian market, Pratap Ghee has been synonymous with ‘Purity’, and it has earned a reputation and heritage for real traditional taste over the years.

We have painstakingly tried to replicate the homely process. According to ayurveda, the traditional procedure produces pure desi ghee which has considerably better flavour, aroma, and nutritional value than ghee that has undergone modern processing.

The Journey

Six Decades of Trust

1958The Beginning of Trust

Sh. Pyare Lal ‘Ghee Wale’ begins the Desi Ghee business from Khari Baoli, Delhi - the historic spice market - with one uncompromising principle: purity above all.

1958 - 2004A Name Synonymous with Purity

Through the consistent efforts of a devoted team and the wholehearted love of customers, Pratap Ghee grows multifold and becomes a name to reckon with across the Indian market.

2004A Spiritual Farewell

Having built a legacy on devotion, the founder takes sanyas from grihastha ashram and spends his remaining years in Rishikesh - the values he lived by still guide every batch we make.

TodayTradition Meets Technology
