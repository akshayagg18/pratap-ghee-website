Our Heritage

A Legacy of Purity, Since 1958

Pratap Ghee is an initiative of Late Sh. Pyare Lal ‘Ghee Wale’ of village Dahar near Panipat, Haryana, who began the Desi Ghee business from Khari Baoli, Delhi in 1958 - with the sole aim of offering India ghee in its purest, finest form.

For over six decades since, Pratap Ghee has cherished its clients’ faith and unwavering trust. In the Indian market it has become synonymous with purity - earning a reputation and heritage for real, traditional taste. We have painstakingly replicated the homely, ayurvedic process which yields ghee of far better flavour, aroma and nutrition than modern methods.

Ghee is Amrit, and adulterating it is the same as poisoning.
Late Sh. Pyare Lal ‘Ghee Wale’, Founder

Why Choose Pratap

Quality. Tradition. Purity.

Every jar carries decades of commitment to the ancient art of ghee-making.

Homely & Authentic

We’ve painstakingly replicated your grandmother’s process - small-batch, slow-cooked, and full of the warmth mass manufacturing simply cannot replicate.

Zero Adulteration

Rooted in our founder’s sacred principle, our production tolerates absolutely no compromise. What leaves our dairy is 100% unadulterated ghee - always.

Nutritionally Rich

Traditional-process ghee is significantly richer in fat-soluble vitamins A, D, E & K, conjugated linoleic acid, and butyric acid than industrially processed alternatives.

Sacred Ritual Purity

Preferred by temples, households and pandits for havans and pujas alike. Pratap Ghee meets the exacting standards of ritual purity our founder always envisioned.

State-of-the-Art Facility

Tradition meets technology at our Sahibabad dairy. Modern hygiene and quality-controlled infrastructure ensure heritage methods are practised with precision.
